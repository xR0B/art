F.A.Q
Please read the frequently asked questions to clarify any doubts before asking me any questions.

IMPORTANT:

If you have arrived here it is because something of my designs/work has caught your attention. So I will clarify the basic question: Why have you increased your prices?, Throughout my facet as an illustrator/programmer I have been developing professional qualities that have given an added value to my work, increasing the demand inside and outside of it. Keep in mind that the price in xats within the scope of "xat.com" sometimes is not even 20% of the real price in USD$. So value the quality that I offer you as well as other designers within this field.

1. What is the difference between a neon background and a normal one?  

Neon: composed of lines with light effects simulating an LED, integrated with bright colors on a dark base surrounded by eye-catching effects.
Normal: composed of graphics based on flat colors, with volume and limited but striking effects.

2. If I order a specific illustration/drawing in the design, will it still cost the same as the base price? 

No. In these general cases I usually illustrate and draw sketches from scratch either in neon or normal mode, so there is an added value to the base price, based on the whole process of complexity needed to reach that quality result.
In the pricing section it is explained in more detail.

 Example: the more elaborate backgrounds on the left would not have the same price as those on the right.  

3. How is the payment process?

You must pay an advance of 50% of the price of the design for me to start it, and the other 50% once it is finished.

4. How many modifications/corrections can I make?

You are entitled to 3 modifications. From then on 150 xats will be charged for each modification because sometimes people abuse. I am more flexible with people who work with me for the first time.

5. How long does it take to deliver the designs?

Based on my availability and order of requests depending on your position on the list I will schedule a specific date for your delivery (adjusted to your convenience). I recommend ordering at least one week in advance.  

6. What if I need an urgent background/design overnight or in a minimum of 2-3 days?

An increase of 30% will be added to the final price of the original work. (Express delivery).

¿Why?
My most valuable resource is time, so don't underestimate the time I invest in prioritizing your order, designing and passing over others, even sometimes over other of my extracurricular activities.

7. Can I have any discount?

Maybe. Each work has its added value in the ideas that I bring and the process of it, and a base price based on its complexity. A possible discount is only at the discretion of the designer.

8. For being an official chat or tribute could you donate it or lower the price?

No. If at some point I decided to donate a design, I would do so of my own accord.

9. Do you sell graphics or editable templates? 

No.

10. Do you sell the PNG of your illustrations in the backgrounds or can you give it to me as a gift?

No. Remember that you are paying for the general design in full high quality of your background on request, but not for the editable art HD or 4K of my illustration to acquire it. However, in some cases I usually have it available in limited size so that you can use it into player designs.

11. If I don't have xats at the moment, can you make the design and I will pay you later? 

No. It is recommended that you wait until you have availability to pay for your order to avoid misunderstandings.

12. If I don't have xats, can I pay for it in real money or vice versa?

Only if you reach an agreement with the designer and he allows you to do so.

13. Can I take your designs to modify them and adapt them to my chat?  

NO, of respect for the designer and the owners who bought the backgrounds.

14. Can you remove your signature from your designs? 

No, it will always be somewhere in the background, usually where everyone already knows.
